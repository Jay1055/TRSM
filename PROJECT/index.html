India is a land of rich cultural heritage and diverse landscapes, making it a top destination for tourists worldwide. With a history spanning thousands of years, the country is home to numerous historical landmarks, including the majestic Taj Mahal, the grand forts of Rajasthan, the ancient temples of Khajuraho, and the rock-cut caves of Ajanta and Ellora. Each site reflects India's architectural brilliance and deep-rooted traditions. From the bustling streets of Delhi to the serene ghats of Varanasi, India's historical treasures offer a captivating glimpse into its glorious past, attracting millions of visitors each year.
